Set up a dashboard for someone

Fill in a person's details, generate their personal link, and text it to them. When they tap it on their own phone or computer, their dashboard fills in automatically — nothing to paste. Their info is carried only inside that link and saved on their device.

  1. Text or email this link to them.
  2. They open it on their own phone or computer — their dashboard fills in automatically.
  3. For the calendar to show events, they sign into their own Google account in that browser.
