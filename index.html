Connect your own Oura & calendars

This dashboard is a static site with no server, so every connection below is either a live keyless feed or a small file you keep updated yourself — nothing that requires storing a private API key in this repo.

Calendars — live, private

  1. In Google Calendar, open Settings for the calendar you want to add.
  2. Under Share with specific people or groups, click Add people and groups and share it with your own Google account email. Do not use "Make available to public" — that shows it to anyone with the link, not just you.
  3. Scroll to Integrate calendar and copy the src= URL from the embed code.
  4. Add it to _data/calendars.yml as a new name / embed_url pair.
  5. The calendar only shows real events to someone signed into that Google account — anyone else sees a blank/permission box.

Weather & UV — live

Fetched automatically from the free, keyless Open-Meteo API. Just set your coordinates once in _data/location.yml.

Oura Ring — live via a free proxy

Oura blocks web pages from calling its API directly, so live sync needs a tiny free relay. Follow the one-time setup in cloudflare-worker/README.md to deploy a Cloudflare Worker (your token is stored securely on your own Cloudflare account, never here), then click Connect Oura above and paste your Worker URL. Prefer not to bother? Just edit today's numbers into _data/oura.yml by hand instead.
