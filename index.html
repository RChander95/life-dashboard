How this stays private

Everything personal on this dashboard is stored only in your own browser, never in the (public) repo. A stranger who opens the link sees an empty shell — no calendar, no numbers, no name. You connect each source once per device.

Calendar — browser only

In Google Calendar → Settings → Integrate calendar, build the embed that includes your Personal and Work calendars (each keeps its own color), then click Connect Calendar above and paste the URL. It's saved only in this browser, and events show only when you're signed into your Google account. Outlook/iCloud can't be shown privately, so they're left off.

Weather & UV — live, private

Uses your browser's location (with your permission) to fetch weather from the free, keyless Open-Meteo API. Your coordinates are never stored or committed.

Oura Ring — live via a free proxy

Oura blocks web pages from calling its API directly, so live sync uses a tiny free relay you deploy once (see cloudflare-worker/README.md). Your token stays on your own Cloudflare account; the dashboard only stores your Worker URL, in this browser. Click Connect Oura above to paste it.
